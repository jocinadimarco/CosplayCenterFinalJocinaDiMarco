Makeup

Makeup can be often overlooked in cosplay but can really help the finished look come together. There are many different styles of makeup that you can use for your cosplays, many taking inspiration for East Asian trends and styles. While you should experiment with what you like and what works best for you, there are a few tips that can help with the general idea.

Don’t be afraid of doing “more”. Most everyday makeup can look nice, but can leave the face looking flat of blank in photos. Especially wild wearing intricate outfits and bold wigs, you want your makeup to be elevated to the same level. This could look like bolder lashes, thicker eyeliner, even darker brows and contouring. With this in mind, it’s not uncommon for men to wear makeup to better replicate the features or elevation of a character. Don’t be afraid to try things out and use some of the endless resources to learn from.
